THE BASEMAPS

We offer several basemap styles. Each is suitable for different purpose. For example if you are interested in road network, the best style for you is one with significant roads. And if you are tourist you should choose basemap with hillshading and contours.

Our basemaps are retina ready so you can enjoy high resolution maps and plans. And of course our basemap is also suitable for print media.

THE MAP STYLER

Modern map symbols

Looking for modern retina map symbols? Are you web developer or cartographer? We create mapkeyicons for you! Visit mapkeyicons.com , download mapkeyicons package and use CSS toolkit for simply usage or download svg or png images. Everything for free! Mapkeyincons are distributed under public domain.

Cartographic/GIS data

We provide plenty GIS data for free! There are mainly based on OpenStreetMap database. In download section you can choose from vector or raster data.

Free GeoTiff maps

We offer rendered geotiff maps and city plans in varying scales and places! Download it and use it for free! Maps are rendered in web mercator (epsg:3857) projection in geotiff format with georeferencing. Ready to be used in your GIS application as basemap.
